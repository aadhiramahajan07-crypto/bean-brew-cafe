Our Story

A Corner Built For Coffee Lovers

Bean & Brew Café was born out of a simple belief — that a great cup of coffee deserves a great setting. Nestled in the heart of Jammu, we opened our doors to create a space where people could slow down, connect, and savour the moment.

From our hand-selected single-origin beans to our house-baked pastries, every detail is crafted with intention. Our baristas train for months to perfect each pour, and our kitchen team brings the same devotion to every plate.

"Coffee is our craft. Your comfort is our purpose."
